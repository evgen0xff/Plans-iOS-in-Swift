# Plans-iOS-in-Swift
Plans iOS in Swift

Plans project, which is a social media application that allows you to make a plan with friends and enjoy them.

https://apps.apple.com/us/app/plans-events-with-friends/id1548425044


Plans makes it easy to expand your social life and keep track of it using a streamlined event calendar. A free social event planner app, Plans makes it simple to be an event organizer, party planner, wedding planner, and more. Use the app to send invitations, create a guest list and meetup with friends or guests at local events. A great way to meet people, meet friends, and build meaningful experiences in real life. Become your own event organizer, meetup with friends and send free invitations today!
